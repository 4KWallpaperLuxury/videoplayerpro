Privacy Policy

Video Player Pro — All-in-One Media Hub

Last Updated: March 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Video Player Pro, developed by GUNGSA STUDIO. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy describes or practices with regards to the information we (and the app) process.

Video Player Pro is designed to be a privacy-first application. Most of its core functionality operates entirely offline, and network communication is strictly limited to features you explicitly interact with.

2. Information Lifecycle & Permissions

Local Media Access

The App requires the following permissions to manage your local entertainment library:

  • Media Access (Video/Audio/Storage): Strictly used to scan, index, and play back files stored on your device.
  • Manage External Storage: Required for the Secure Vault and file management (rename/delete). This allows the app to move your private media to a protected internal directory.

YouTube & Streaming

When you use the YouTube search or playback featured, the App interacts with third-party APIs (via NewPipeExtractor) to fetch public video metadata and stream links.

  • No Tracking: We do not collect your search queries or watch history.
  • Anonymized Requests: Network requests are made directly to media instances, and we enforce encrypted HTTPS communication for all such requests.

IPTV Service

IPTV playlist URLs and stream data are processed locally on your device. We do not transmit your playlist contents or streaming activity to our servers.

3. Secure Vault & Privacy Protection

The Vault feature provides an additional layer of security for your sensitive media:

  • Local Pin Hashing: Your access PIN is protected using SHA-256 industry-standard hashing. We never store or transmit your actual PIN.
  • Isolation: Files moved to the Vault are stored in the App's private internal storage, making them invisible to galleries and other applications.
  • No Cloud Backups: To maximize privacy, Vault data is excluded from automated Android cloud backups. Your private files stay strictly on your physical device.

4. Network Security

We implement a strict Network Security Configuration that prohibits cleartext (HTTP) traffic. All data transmitted by the app (for YouTube info, subtitles, or streams) is sent over encrypted TLS (HTTPS) channels to prevent "man-in-the-middle" attacks and ensure your connection safety.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy frequently.
