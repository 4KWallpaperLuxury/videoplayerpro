Privacy Policy

Video Player Pro — All-in-One Media Hub

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Video Player Pro, developed by GUNGSA STUDIO. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us.

When you use our mobile application ("App"), you trust us with your personal information. We take your privacy very seriously. In this privacy policy, we describe our privacy practices to explain to you in the clearest way possible what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

2. Information We Collect

Local Storage and Permissions

Video Player Pro is primarily a local media player. To function properly, the App requires certain permissions to access files on your device:

  • Read Media/Storage: Used strictly to scan, catalog, and playback video and audio files stored locally on your device.
  • Manage External Storage (All Files Access): Required for the "Secure Vault" and file deletion features, allowing the App to securely move, hide, or delete media files upon your explicit request.

IPTV URLs & Playlists

If you use the IPTV streaming feature, the App processes the URLs you provide locally on your device. We do not track, collect, or store your IPTV playlists or streaming habits on our external servers.

3. Secure Vault & Data Security

We use local PIN encryption and randomized file naming (UUIDs) to protect files moved to the "Secure Vault". These files are isolated within the App's private directory to prevent them from appearing in your device's gallery or being scanned by other applications.

Important: Your Vault PIN and the actual media files are stored locally on your device. We do not back up your Vault PIN or files. If you forget your PIN or uninstall the App without restoring your Vault files first, that data may be permanently lost.

4. Third-Party Services & Analytics

Video Player Pro is designed with privacy in mind. Currently, the App operates locally and does not actively transmit your private media usage data, viewing history, or personal identifiers to third-party tracking services or analytics platforms. Any network requests made by the App are exclusively for:

  • Fetching remote IPTV streams requested by you.
  • Retrieving remote subtitles (if applicable and requested).

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date and the updated version will be effective as soon as it is accessible. We encourage you to review this privacy policy frequently to be informed of how we are protecting your information.
